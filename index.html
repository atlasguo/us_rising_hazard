Welcome to the United States of Rising Hazards!

· This is an interactive edition of the award-winning static map The United States of the Blooming Risks. Using FEMA's National Risk Index (NRI) dataset, this map aggregates county-level risk scores for 18 natural hazards into 1K-square-mile hexagons across the 50 states and builds a multivariate visualization with layer toggling and spatial query (clicking or searching).

· Note that the map might load slowly due to numerous layers with heavy visual effects, so it is not recommended for mobile users.
