Welcome to the United States of Rising Hazards!

· This is an interactive edition of the award-winning static map United States of Blooming Risks.

· Using FEMA's National Risk Index (NRI) dataset, this map aggregates county-level risk scores for 18 natural hazards into 1K-square-mile hexagons across the 50 states and builds a multivariate visualization with layer toggling and spatial query.

· This map is designed by Atlas Guo (2025), CartoGuophy.com. View on desktop for best experience. To get detailed information about this map, visit the introductive page.
